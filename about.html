Our Journey

From Vision to Vanguard.

A timeline of milestones that mark our pursuit of engineering excellence and aerospace credibility.

2021 — 2023

Foundation of Expertise

Co-founders begin deep R&D in autonomous flight, accumulating multi-year technical expertise in flight control, perception, and aerospace systems.

2024 — 2025

Global Recognition

Winners of SUAS (International, USA) and Aerothon (National) UAV competitions — validating Verniq capability on the world stage.

2025

First Specialized Order

Verniq UAVs founded. Secured our first specialized customer order within six months of company establishment.

2026

Delivery & Expansion

Successfully delivered first contract platform; partnered with premier institutes including Nirma University for advanced research.

March 2026

First International Defence Contract

Completed first international contract — ISR surveillance UAV developed for Bjerkwick Defence (Norway), marking Verniq's entry into the global defence market.
