Engineering Process

From mission requirement
to field-ready aircraft.

Every Verniq platform is engineered through a disciplined eight-stage workflow — spanning requirements capture, design, integration, simulation, and field validation.

01

Mission Requirement

Operational profile, payload, environment, regulatory framework.

02

Airframe Design

Aero modelling, CFD, structural analysis, CAD & weight budget.

03

Avionics Integration

FCU, ESCs, sensors, datalink — wiring & EMC discipline.

04

Flight Control Tuning

Control loops, autopilot parameters, fail-safes, geo-fencing.

05

Payload Integration

EO/IR, LiDAR, sprayer, RF — mechanical, electrical, software.

06

Simulation & HITL

SITL/HITL flight rehearsals, autonomy regression, edge-case fuzzing.

07

Field Validation

Tethered → free-flight → mission profile → environmental qualification.

08

Deployment

Operator training, documentation handover, lifecycle support.
